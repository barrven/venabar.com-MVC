- Mike is exploring virtual reality (VR) and shares his experience of flying Superman-style over Manhattan using VR.
- He highlights the exciting capabilities of VR, mentioning its applications in gaming as well as military, medical, and engineering fields.
- Mike uses the HTC Vive Pro VR system, which includes controllers with sensors, a headset with OLED monitors, and infrared transmitters for precise tracking.
- He explains the difference between virtual reality, where users experience a fully virtual world, and augmented reality (AR), which overlays digital elements onto the real world.
- Examples of augmented reality include apps like Instagram and Google Translate that enhance real-world interaction with digital information.
- He encourages others to experience VR for themselves.
